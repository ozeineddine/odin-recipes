Overnight Oats

Description:

This is a classic overnight oats recipe that I have perfected over the span of a couple of months. You will need 8 ingredients (1 is optional.) It takes only a couple of minutes to make, but it will have to be stored in the fridge for a minumum of 4 hours to achieve the best results.

Ingredients:

For the Oats:

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter (or 1 serving powdered peanut butter)
  • 1/4 cup greek yogurt (or substitute with favorite yogurt)
  • 1/2 cup liquid of choice (milk, water, etc.)
  • 1 Banana
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup or sweetener of choice
  • OPTIONAL: 1 scoop vanilla protein powder

For the Toppings:

  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Combine first 4 ingredients, mix well.
  2. Slice the banana into small pieces, add them to oats.
  3. Add your sweetener, and protein powder if desired.
  4. Add your toppings, can add more fruit if desired.
  5. Cover, and leave in fridge for 4 hours or overnight.

Enjoy!
